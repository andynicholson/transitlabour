Mailing lists


Transit list

Transit-labour mailing lists are tools for bridging between the project's online and offline aspects. Three lists will be used to link the development of online materials to the organization of platforms and events.

This public list is the general discussion, debate and announcement list for the Transit Labour project. It will be used for discussion associated with the three Transit Labour platforms: Shanghai (2010), Kolkata (2011) and Sydney (2012).

Platform list

This is an organization list used to plan the platforms in Shanghai (2010), Kolkata (2011) and Sydney (2012). List members will change as the project moves across space and time.

For further information contact the list owner/mailto:ned@nedrossiter.org

Project list

This is a communication list used to send project updates to supporting organizations, researchers and advisors linked to the Transit Labour project.

For further information contact the list owner/mailto:ned@nedrossiter.org
